Methodology Financial figures (committed spend, top suppliers, budget-vs-actual) and PO counts shown on this dashboard both exclude drafts. Drafts are procurement intent, not commitment — AM auto-saves partial form state when a user abandons a PO, producing stale records that don't represent real spend or real decisions. The "Total POs" figure is the count of PO decisions (approved, on-approval, rejected, cancelled). Drafts are shown below as a governance signal to keep periodically clean.
